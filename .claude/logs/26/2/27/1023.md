# application/required 도메인별 패키징

- `required/favorite/` (4개): FavoriteAdder, FavoriteChecker, FavoriteLister, FavoriteRemover

- `required/importance/` (3개): ImportanceGetter, ImportanceRemover, ImportanceSetter

- `required/newsgroup/` (7개): NewsGroupCreator, NewsGroupDeleter, NewsGroupFinder, NewsGroupLister, NewsGroupNewsAdder, NewsGroupNewsRemover, NewsGroupUpdater
- `required/opinion/` (4개): OpinionCreator, OpinionFinder, OpinionLister, OpinionUpdater
